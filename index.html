About us

Climate Business Collective brings together people worried about climate change and who want to take action. We believe that power to make the change lies within individuals. By creating sustainable services and products we can empower individuals to make better decisions for themselves and for climate.

Events

Events bring people together to discuss and brainstorm about services and products that can help individuals to make better decisions in their daily lives.

Every event has a theme that we are going to dive into deeper with a company or a specialist from the field.

Events are free to attend. All you need is a little bit of curiosity.
